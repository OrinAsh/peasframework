PEAS: Public Equity Allocation System

A Framework for Shared Wealth, Civic Governance, and Long-Term Stewardship

What is PEAS?

The Public Equity Allocation System (PEAS) is a democratic post-capitalist model that replaces debt-driven inequality with population-indexed dividends, civic-owned wealth, and participatory governance through juries, stewards, and transparent petition systems. It is designed to evolve over generations and enable intergenerational justice without upheaval.
