Public Equity Allocation System (PEAS)

A Framework for Shared Wealth, Civic Governance, and Long-Term Stewardship

What is PEAS?

PEAS is a post-capitalist economic and governance model that eliminates dynastic ownership, redistributes wealth equitably, and enables participatory governance through jury-based and steward-guided systems. It is designed for long-term adaptability and intergenerational justice.
